Mahavir Swami ki jay jay,
Ahimsa ke prateek tum ho.

Karm bandhan tod diya tumne,
Moksh ka marg dikhaya hai.
Jag ko satya ka paath padhaya,
Anekantvad sikhaya hai.

Veer tumhari jay jay hove,
Tum bin jag andhiyara hai.
Kshama karo he karunasagar,
Tum hi to ek sahara ho.

Mahavir Swami ki jay jay,
Ahimsa ke prateek tum ho.
